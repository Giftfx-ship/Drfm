About Divine Reconciliation

Divine Reconciliation Faith Ministries began as a small band of faithful worshippers who were hungry for God and committed to seeing lives transformed by the power of the Gospel. Today we are a multi-generational fellowship that prioritizes the presence of God, the preaching of Jesus Christ, and practical demonstrations of God’s love in our city and nation.

Our weekly services are saturated with passionate praise, fervent prayer, and expository preaching that leads people into a deeper knowledge of the Word. We value discipleship, deliverance, and the equipping of saints for service. We are committed to training leaders, supporting families, and advancing the kingdom through worship and witness.

We welcome everyone: the seeker, the skeptic, the broken, the joyful — all are invited to taste and see that the Lord is good. Come and experience an atmosphere of love, healing, and powerful ministry that fuels personal and community transformation.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To see lives reconciled to God, families restored, and communities transformed. We envision a movement of believers who carry the fire of revival into every sphere of society — homes, schools, markets, and government.

Mission

  1. Preach the unadulterated Gospel of Jesus Christ, calling people to repentance and faith.
  2. Raise disciples who are rooted in Scripture, empowered by the Holy Spirit, and equipped for ministry.
  3. Engage in strategic outreach to meet physical and spiritual needs in our community.
  4. Train leaders and champions who will steward the church into greater fruitfulness.

What We Believe

We believe in one God — Father, Son and Holy Spirit. We believe in the authority of Scripture, the atoning death and resurrection of Jesus Christ, and the baptism of the Holy Spirit for empowerment to live a holy and fruitful life. We affirm prayer, prophetic ministry, and the exercise of spiritual gifts for the edification of the church.

Our doctrine centers on salvation by grace through faith, the sanctifying work of the Spirit, and the expectation of Christ’s return. We are committed to sound teaching and the pursuit of holiness, compassion, justice and mercy.

Ministries

A dynamic, multi-layered worship experience every Sunday by 9:00 AM: congregational worship, choir anthems, ministry time, and anointed preaching. Families and children are well catered for with heartwarming programmes.

Midweek Word & Prayer

A time to dive into the Word and pray in power — a midweek replenishment for believers hungry for growth.

Youth & NextGen

Vibrant sessions designed to disciple and mobilize young people, helping them discover purpose and lead with integrity.

Women of Valor

A fellowship empowering women through teaching, mentorship, and outreach so they can thrive in family and ministry.

Men's Fellowship

Strengthening men to lead in family, church and business with integrity, courage and godly wisdom.

Evangelism & Outreach

Regular outreach programs that bring hope — food distributions, home visits, prison ministry, and community support.
